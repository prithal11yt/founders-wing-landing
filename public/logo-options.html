Founders Wing — Network Logo Concepts

All 6 options built around the idea of connection, nodes, and networks.

Option 1 — Constellation
Founders Wing

Connected nodes forming a constellation — each founder is a point of light, connected to the network. The top node (you) is brightest. Organic, not corporate.

Option 2 — Hub & Spoke
Founders Wing

Classic network topology — a strong center hub with 7 connected members. Clear hierarchy: the community connects everyone through a shared core. Bold and structured.

Option 3 — Wing Mesh
Founders Wing

Network nodes arranged in a wing shape — the "wing" IS the network. Nodes fade smaller as they extend outward. Combines both brand concepts: wing + network. Best of both worlds.

Option 4 — Delta Network
Founders Wing

Triangular network — stability, strength, interconnection. Every node connects to every other. Feels structured and intentional, like a curated community should. Geometric and clean.

Option 5 — Growth Network
Founders Wing

Network expanding outward in rings — shows growth and reach. Inner circle is tight-knit (the 50 members), outer ring is the extended network. Captures the "small but powerful" positioning.

Option 6 — Hex Network
Founders Wing

Hexagonal structure — the strongest shape in nature. Six founders connected through a central hub. Signals: structure, efficiency, community engineering. Clean and techy.
